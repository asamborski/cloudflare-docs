## Set up your domain

Before using Cloudflare Email Service, you need to configure your domain for email sending and routing.

### Onboard your domain

1. In the Cloudflare dashboard, go to **Email Sending**.

   <DashButton url="/?to=/:account/email-service/sending" />

2. Select **Onboard Domain**.

3. Choose a domain from your Cloudflare account.

4. Select **Continue** to proceed with DNS configuration.

5. Select **Add records and onboard**. This will add the following DNS records to your domain:
   - **Sending records** on the `cf-bounce` subdomain: MX records for bounce handling, TXT records for SPF and DKIM authentication.
   - **Routing records** on the root domain: MX records to route incoming emails to Cloudflare, TXT records for SPF and DKIM authentication.

:::note[DNS Propagation]
DNS changes can take up to 24 hours to propagate globally, but usually complete within 5-15 minutes for domains using Cloudflare DNS.
:::

Once your domain is verified, you can start sending and routing emails.
